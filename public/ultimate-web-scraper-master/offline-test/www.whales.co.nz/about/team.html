Mike

Senior Pilot

You may know Mike from such works as; that extra scene for 2 seconds in the background of Shortland Street on Season 4 episode 6 at 12:32 minutes. Also for his impressive role as “guard 4” on Netflix’s Roman Empire. When Mike is not flying, he is often seen wooing the crowds at the local pub playing guitar and singing. His voice has been described by many as an angel with a slight toothache. Since joining Wings Over Whales, Mike has become a huge whale enthusiast, and has even travelled to Tonga to swim with humpback whales. The rest of the pilots will be very grateful if you direct all of your whale related questions to Mike.

Emily

Pilot

Emily grew up as a grape picker on her family’s vineyard in beautiful Blenheim. Her small and nimble fingers quickly earned her the reputation of the fastest picker in the Marlborough region. Seeking bigger thrills in her teen years, Emily applied her hand-eye coordination skills into the world of sport and became a master of hockey. Emily’s ambition to become an Instagram star soon lead her to swapping a hockey stick for control stick in an aeroplane. Emily has been with Wings Over Whales for over two years now and is loving her time here. She believes the best selfies will be from the cockpit of a jet aircraft so one day hopes to progress to the airlines.

 

Daniel

Owner and Operator

Daniel's flying career began in 2004 right here in Kaikoura, taking tourists out whale watching by helicopter. He then went on to further his experience overseas flying in Canada, Papua New Guinea and Indonesia. However the pull back to Kaikoura was too strong and in 2016 the opportunity arose for Daniel and his wife Monique to start their own whale watching business here in Kaikoura; South Pacific Helicopters. Flash forward to 2018 and they were given the opportunity to add Wings Over Whales to their aviation family. Daniel can be found flying the helicopters daily, however he leaves the fixed wings to the other pilots! 

Monique Stevenson

Owner and Operator

Business and life partner to Daniel, Monique is a local Marlborough girl who was brought up just up the road in Blenheim, the heart of New Zealand’s famous wine region. She brings a wealth of knowledge to Wings Over Whales through her years spent in the customer service industry while owning her own successful beauty clinic for the past 15 years.

Monique is our busy bee behind the scenes.  When shes not in the office she is busy keeping things running smoothly at home and helping bring up their two young boys Tommy and Johnno.

Krissy is a Canterbury girl born in Christchurch. She spend time overseas in Japan, UK, Europe and the US, working in tourism throughout.
